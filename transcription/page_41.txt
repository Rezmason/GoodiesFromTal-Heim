                Hello Dolly Cookies

Melt 1 stick of oleo in 9 x 13 inch pan you are
going to bake in.  2 cups graham cracker crumbs
sprinkled evenly in melted oleo.  1 6 oz. pkg.
of chocolate chips, sprinkled over crumbs.
1/2 cup chopped nuts, sprinkle evenly on top.
1 can Bakers angel flake coconut sprinkled on
top.  Pour one can of Eagle Brand milk over this.
Do not stir.  Bake at 325Â° for 30 mtes.
