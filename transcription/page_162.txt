              PICKLED ASPARAGUS

1/3 c. vinegar             1 stick cinnamon
1/4 c. water               ¼ t. celery seed
1/4 c. sugar
½ t. salt
3 - 12 whole cloves

Boil to blend flavors.  Rour over two cans
asparagus in deep dish.  Let stand several
hours and chill. Double for 4 cans of green
asparagus.

                            Celesta True
