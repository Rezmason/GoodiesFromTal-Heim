           PEANUT BRITTLE MOUSSE
                        (very old recipe)

1 pint whipping cream  (whipped)
½ lb. peanut brittle
    (beat peanut brittle in blender for just
     a second)
½ lb. marhmellows cut small
         (Just use small ones)

Combine above and serve in goblets or parfait
glasses.

                          Rosie Netterville
