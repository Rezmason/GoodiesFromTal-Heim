                      TUNA JELLO SALAD

1 pk. lemon Jello            3 boiled eggs
1½ c. boiling water          3 T. lemon juice
1 c. celery                  1 c. mayonnaise
½ c. green olives            2 small cans tuna
½ c. sweet pickles

Mix Jello mixture and tuna mixture and pour into
mold.  Refrigerate.

                                      Mrs. Don Cox
