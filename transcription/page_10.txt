                HOT CRAB DIP

1 10 oz. pkg sharp cheddar cheese
1  8 oz. pkg sliced sharp process cheddar cheese
½ stick of butter
½ cup of sauterne
1 can crabmeat or 8 oz. frozen

Over low heat melt butter and cheeses stirring
constantly, add sauterne and crab meat, transfer
to chafing dish.  Serve with Euphrate sesame
wafers and Triscuit crackers.
