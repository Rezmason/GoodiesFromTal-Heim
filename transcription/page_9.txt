                CHEESE WAFERS

½ lb. sharp cheese, grated  1½ c. flour
¼ lb. butter                ½ c. nuts
1 t. salt                   Red Pepper (optional
                                       to taste)
Cream butter and cheese. Add salt, pepper, flour.
Shape into roll, chill overnight. Slice thin.
Bake in Hot oven 375 degrees for 15 minutes.

                            Rosie Netterville
