            NO-BAKE COOKIES

Step I
2 c. sugar              ¼ c. cocoa
1 stick oleo            ½ c. milk

Combine in saucepan and boil for 1 minute. Take
off stove.

Step II
Add and stir into the first mixture in saucepan
½ c. peanut butter, 1 t. vanilla, and 3 cups
oatmeal (uncooked quick oats). Drop by teaspoons
on wax paper.

                              Carolyn Segrest
