                  TUNA CASSEROLE

1 can mushroom soup         ¼ c. chopped onion
1 can tuna                  1 c. chopped celery
¼ c. cashew nuts            1 can Chinese noodles(3½oz.)
¼ c. water                  Pepper & salt

Mix, reserving ½ of noodles for top.  Bake at 325
degrees for 40 minutes.
                                  Mrs. Barbara Kacker
