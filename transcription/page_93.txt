                        PECAN PIE

3 eggs                       ¼ c. butter
2 T. flour                   Dash salt
½ c. white syrup             ½ c. pecans

Pour into uncooked pie shell and bake 10 min. at
375 degrees.  Reduce to 350 degrees and bake 50
min.  Yield- 1 pie.
                                    Bettie Braswell
