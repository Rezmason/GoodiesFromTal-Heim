                    Sillouette Salad

Serves 4 - 140 calories per serving

1 env. unflavored gel.       1 cup water
1 - 10-1/2 oz. can cream     1 tabl. lemon juice
 of chicken soup             dash black pepper
1 - 5 oz. can boned chicken  1/2 cup chopped celery
    or turkey or leftovers
1/4 cup chopped green pepper 2 tabl. chopped pimento
2 tabl. grated onion

Sprinkle gelatin on 1/2 cup of the water to soften.
Place over low heat and stir until gelatin is
dissolved.  Remove from heat and blend in soup until
smooth - add remaining 1/2 cup water, lemon juice
