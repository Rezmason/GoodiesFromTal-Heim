                  Cantonese Beef Balls

Preheat oven to 350Â°.  Combine 1 lb. ground chuck,
1/4 cup chopped onions, 1 teas. salt, 1/2 teas.
pepper and 1/2 cup milk.  Form meat into 20 small
balls and arrange in baking pan and bake for 35
to 40 minutes.

Meanwhile, combine 1/4 cup sugar and 1-1/2 tabl.
cornstarch in a sauce pan.  Stir in slowly 7/8
cup pineapple juice.  Add 1/4 cup vinegar, 2 tabl.
water, 1 teas. soy sauce and 1 tabl. butter.
Cook over low heat until clear, stirring constantly.
Remove from heat and set aside.  Heat 2 tabl.
butter in a small skillet.  Add 1/2 cup blanched
almonds.  Fry slowly until golden; set aside.
