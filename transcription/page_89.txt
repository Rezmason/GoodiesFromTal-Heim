               GRASSHOPPER PIE

Crust: 1½ c. chocolate cookies crushed
       ½ c. butter
Combine and chill
In double boiler melt 20 marshmellows & ½ c milk
Chill well in a bowl.
Whip 1 cup whipping cream add 3 T. creme de
menthe, 3 T. creme de cocoa. Fold in marsh-
mellow mixture. Fill pie shell and chill.

                             Celesta True
